LivE Platform — User Rules & Guidelines (Viewers)

Welcome to LivEstates. To ensure a professional, efficient, and fair live-streaming experience for all users, we have established the following rules and guidelines for users. By using our platform, you agree to abide by these rules.

1. Age and Eligibility Requirements

LivEstates requires users to be at least 13 years old to use the app.

2. Behavior Guidelines

  • No Harassment or Bullying
    Viewers must not harass, insult, threaten, or provoke other users. This includes posting malicious comments, verbal attacks, or engaging in provocative behavior towards the streamer or other viewers.
  • No Malicious Activities
    Viewers are prohibited from engaging in spam, flooding the chat with repeated messages, or participating in “bot” behavior. Malicious actions can lead to temporary muting or permanent account bans.
  • Use Respectful Language
    Viewers must use polite language and refrain from posting racist, sexist, violent, pornographic, or hate speech. These rules apply to the comment section as well.

3. Content Viewing Guidelines

  • Follow Content Guidelines
    Viewers should not encourage or request the streamer to engage in behavior that violates platform rules. If viewers observe inappropriate content, they should report it through the proper reporting process.
  • No Copyright Infringement
    Viewers must not record, download, or redistribute live stream content without permission. This includes illegally recording or sharing a streamer’s content.

4. Streaming Etiquette for Viewers

  • Respecting Streamer’s Time and Effort
    Viewers should acknowledge the effort and time that streamers put into their live content. Engaging in a positive and constructive manner can create a more enjoyable experience for everyone.
  • Respect the Streamer’s Boundaries
    Viewers should respect the streamer’s boundaries and avoid making inappropriate demands, requests, or comments. This includes refraining from pushing a streamer to disclose personal information or engage in activities they’re not comfortable with.
  • Viewers should not support or encourage harmful trends, such as bullying, harassment, or risky challenges, either within the stream or in chat. These actions can create a toxic environment that undermines the platform’s safety and community goals.

5. User Privacy and Data Protection

  • Platform Data Collection and Use
    Platforms may collect data from viewers, including viewing history, interactions, and location. Viewers should understand and accept the platform’s privacy policy to protect their personal information.
  • Privacy Protection
    Viewers should not invade others’ privacy, such as by recording streams without consent, taking screenshots, or sharing personal information about other viewers.

6. Community Engagement and Content Updates

Platforms regularly update content creation and viewing rules. Viewers should stay informed and comply with any new guidelines to ensure their actions remain compliant.

7. Local Law Compliance

In addition to the platform’s rules, viewers should ensure they comply with local laws regarding online conduct.

8. Report & Support

If you have any questions regarding these rules or observe any individual engaging in conduct that violates our policies, please contact or report the incident to us through our service team at info@livestates.tech.

Reporting and Addressing Violations

  • Report Violations — If a viewer notices other users violating platform rules (e.g., harassment, inappropriate content), they should use the platform’s reporting feature to flag the violation. The platform will take appropriate actions, such as muting or banning accounts.
  • Monitor Live Content — Platforms use automated tools and manual reviews to monitor live content and comment sections. If viewers encounter inappropriate content, they can report it to help the platform address the issue.
  • Real-Time Safety Measures — In the event of an emergency or inappropriate content, platforms may provide instant actions (such as blocking or muting a viewer) to protect both the streamer and the audience.
