LivE Platform — User Rules & Guidelines (Streamers)

Welcome to LivEstates. To ensure a professional, efficient, and fair live-streaming experience for all users, we have established the following rules and guidelines for hosting live showings. By using our platform, you agree to abide by these rules.

1. Eligibility Requirements

Age Restrictions: Users must be at least 18 years old to host a live stream on LivEstates.

2. Community Standards & General Guidelines

  • The platform is strictly for live streaming real estate showings, real estate tour video uploads, or related educational discussions.
  • All content must be relevant to real estate and adhere to community standards.
  • No false advertising, misleading property descriptions, or unverified claims.
  • Hosts must engage professionally and respect viewers. Encouraging a positive and respectful chat environment is expected.
  • The use of copyrighted materials without permission is not allowed.
  • Harassment, hate speech, or inappropriate behavior is strictly prohibited.
  • No sexually explicit content, inappropriate behavior, or suggestive material.
  • No racist, sexist, or other discriminatory remarks.
  • No depiction of violence, criminal acts, or any promotion of illegal substances.
  • No content that encourages self-harm, dangerous stunts, or unsafe activities.
  • No spreading of false information or fraudulent schemes.
  • Live streaming features might not be available in all countries due to local regulations.

3. Live Streaming Rules

  • Punctuality: Users must start their live stream at the scheduled time.
  • Cancellation Policy: If a live stream needs to be canceled, the host must publish a cancellation announcement at least 2 hours in advance.
  • Missed Live Streams: If a host fails to start their scheduled live stream without prior cancellation, their account rating may decrease. Three or more unexcused absences may result in account downgrade to a standard user account. If your account is downgraded, you will need to apply for an upgrade back to the professional account. The platform will review and process your request as soon as possible.

4. Best Practices for a Successful Live Stream

  • Plan content in advance to engage your audience.
  • Use appropriate background settings and ensure a stable internet connection and clear audio/video quality.
  • Avoid disruptions such as excessive buffering, background noise, or technical issues.

5. Hosting Educational or Informational Sessions

  • Submit a topic proposal to LivEstates’s customer service team at least 1 day before the scheduled stream.
  • Wait for approval before setting up the live stream.
  • Clearly outline the session's topic, objectives, and relevance to real estate.

6. Compliance and Enforcement

  • Breaking the rules may result in warnings, temporary or permanent suspension of live streaming privileges. Severe violations can lead to account bans or legal consequences based on platform policies and local laws.
  • The LivEstates team reserves the right to review, modify, or remove any content that does not comply with these guidelines.

7. Privacy & Security

  • Personal Information Protection — Do not share sensitive details or financial information.
  • Guest & Viewer Privacy — If featuring guests, obtain their permission before going live. Do not reveal private details about others without consent.
  • Platforms may collect data from streamers, including streaming history, interactions, and location. Streamers should understand and accept the platform’s privacy policy to protect their personal information.

8. Report & Support

If you have any questions regarding these rules or observe any individual engaging in conduct that violates our policies, please contact or report the incident to us through our service team at info@livestates.tech.

Moderation Tools

  • Platforms provide features to block users, mute comments, and filter inappropriate words.
  • Hosts are encouraged to use these tools to maintain a safe streaming experience.
  • Reporting Violations — Use platform reporting features to flag rule-breaking content or behavior.
  • Dealing with Online Harassment — If you experience harassment, block the user, report the incident, and consider restricting chat access.
  • Crisis Support — If encountering a viewer expressing distress (e.g., self-harm concerns), direct them to appropriate helplines or report to the platform.
