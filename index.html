LivEstates

LivEstates is a brand new social media platform dedicated to revolutionizing traditional property presenting experience in the real estate industry. We offer an opportunity that allows users to interact with creators to access a variety of properties in real-time via livestreams, which will definitely improve efficiency and convenience on both sides. Moreover, our product aims to connect all individuals related to real estate, including buyers, sellers, agents, lenders, even builders and much more. With the state-of-the-art technology, we provide a seamless experience for our users to obtain all property-related information at their fingertips!
